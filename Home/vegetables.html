VEGETABLES

Discover Sri Lanka's Bounty: A Culinary Journey Through Local Vegetable Venues

Misty Mountain Farms, Nuwara Eliya

Perched atop the misty hills of Nuwara Eliya, Misty Mountain Farms is a picturesque retreat where vegetables thrive in the cool, crisp air and fertile soil. Surrounded by emerald tea plantations and rolling meadows, this idyllic farm is home to a bounty of fresh produce, from crunchy broccoli to vibrant bell peppers and sweet strawberries. Each vegetable is nurtured with care and precision, reflecting the natural beauty and abundance of the surrounding landscape, making Misty Mountain Farms a cherished source of wholesome goodness for locals and visitors alike.

Golden Fields Farm, Dambulla

Located amidst the ancient ruins and lush countryside of Dambulla, Golden Fields Farm is a treasure trove of fresh vegetables and flavors. Here, amidst the golden hues of the countryside and the rich history of the region, farmers cultivate a diverse array of produce, from crisp lettuce to juicy tomatoes and fragrant herbs. With its fertile soil and abundant sunshine, Golden Fields Farm is renowned for its exceptional quality and taste, offering a taste of the rich agricultural heritage of Sri Lanka.

Upland Orchards, Badulla

Nestled in the verdant hills of Badulla, Upland Orchards is a serene retreat where vegetables flourish in the cool mountain air and fertile volcanic soil. Surrounded by lush forests and cascading waterfalls, this idyllic orchard is home to a variety of fresh produce, from tender spinach to crunchy carrots and aromatic spices. Each vegetable is grown with care and dedication, reflecting the natural beauty and bounty of the region, making Upland Orchards a beloved destination for locals and tourists seeking a taste of Sri Lanka's agricultural heritage.
